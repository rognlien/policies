# Privacy Policy

**Drop a Crumb** — Last updated: 21 February 2026

## Overview

Drop a Crumb is a location-saving app that stores data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data.

## Data We Access

- **Location** — Used to save the places you choose to remember. Location data is stored only on your device.
- **Camera** — Optionally used to attach a photo to a saved location. Photos are stored only on your device.

## Third-Party Services

- **Apple Maps / Reverse Geocoding** — When you save a location, your coordinates are sent to Apple to resolve a street address. This is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## Data Storage

All saved locations and photos are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not operate servers and have no access to your data.

## Analytics and Tracking

We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or tracking of any kind.
